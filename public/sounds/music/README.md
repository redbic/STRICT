# Background Music Directory

Place your `.ogg` music files here:

## Required Files

- **combat_theme.ogg** - Main gameplay background music
  - Recommended: Action/adventure loop, moderate tempo
  - Duration: 60-180 seconds (will loop seamlessly)
  - Format: Stereo, 44.1kHz, 128-192kbps
  - **Important**: Ensure loop point is seamless (fade out/in or natural loop)

## Optional Files

- **menu_theme.ogg** - Menu/lobby background music (future enhancement)
  - Recommended: Ambient, calmer than combat theme
  - Format: Stereo, 44.1kHz, 128-192kbps

## Audio Specifications

- **Format**: `.ogg` (Ogg Vorbis)
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- **Bitrate**: 128-192kbps for music (higher than SFX)
- **Channels**: Stereo (for fuller sound)
- **Loop-Friendly**: Must loop without audible gap or click

## Loop Tips

1. **Natural Loop**: Compose music that naturally cycles back to start
2. **Crossfade**: Fade out last 1-2 seconds, fade in first 1-2 seconds
3. **Use Audacity**: "Generate > Crossfade Loop" to create seamless loops
4. **Test**: Listen for clicks/pops at loop boundary
