# Sound Effects Directory

Place your `.ogg` sound effect files here:

## Required Files

- **gun_fire.ogg** - Pistol gunshot sound
  - Recommended: Sharp, punchy gunshot
  - Duration: 0.2-0.5 seconds
  - Format: Mono or Stereo, 44.1kHz, 128-192kbps

- **impact.ogg** - Bullet hitting enemy sound
  - Recommended: Metal/flesh impact, thud sound
  - Duration: 0.1-0.3 seconds
  - Format: Mono preferred, 44.1kHz, 128-192kbps

- **enemy_hurt.ogg** - Enemy damage grunt/squeal
  - Recommended: Pain vocalization, grunt, or creature sound
  - Duration: 0.2-0.6 seconds
  - Format: Mono preferred, 44.1kHz, 128-192kbps

- **enemy_death.ogg** - Enemy death sound
  - Recommended: Final death cry or explosion
  - Duration: 0.4-1.0 seconds
  - Format: Mono or Stereo, 44.1kHz, 128-192kbps

## Audio Specifications

- **Format**: `.ogg` (Ogg Vorbis)
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- **Bitrate**: 128-192kbps
- **Channels**: Mono for SFX (saves bandwidth), Stereo acceptable
- **Normalization**: Peak normalize to -3dB to prevent clipping
